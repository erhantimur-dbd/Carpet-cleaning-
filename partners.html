How It Works

We bring the clients. You bring the craft.

Elevate Carpet Care generates more demand than we can handle alone. Our partner programme connects skilled independent cleaners with overflow jobs in their area.

01

Apply & Get Vetted

Submit your details below. We review your experience, insurance, and equipment to ensure you meet our quality standard.

02

Receive Job Offers

When jobs come in for your area, we send them to you first. Accept the ones that fit your schedule — no obligation.

03

Complete the Work

Deliver the clean to Elevate standards. We provide checklists, before/after photo requirements, and customer briefings.

04

Get Paid Promptly

We handle all client billing. You receive your agreed rate within 7 days of job completion — no chasing invoices.

05

Build Your Reputation

Top-rated partners get priority job allocation and higher volume. Your quality directly grows your earnings.

06

Stay Independent

You're not an employee. Keep your own clients, set your availability, and choose which jobs to accept.

What You Get

Built for independent operators.

  • Steady flow of pre-booked, pre-paid jobs
  • No marketing spend — clients come to you
  • Guaranteed payment within 7 days
  • Branded support materials and checklists
  • Priority allocation for top-rated partners
  • Access to commercial and landlord contracts
  • Optional use of Elevate branding on-site
  • Dedicated partner support contact

What We Need

Our standards are non-negotiable.

  • Minimum 2 years carpet cleaning experience
  • Professional-grade extraction equipment
  • Public liability insurance (minimum £1M)
  • Own transport with equipment capacity
  • Smartphone for job updates and photos
  • DBS check (or willingness to obtain one)
  • Commitment to Elevate quality standards
  • Professional appearance and communication
